# UK Company Verification Skill

This skill allows the Legal Compliance & Onboarding Agent to verify a UK company's registration details, directors, and filing history, typically via the Companies House API or other public verification sources.

## Usage
1. Provide the UK Company Registration Number (CRN) or the exact Company Name.
2. The agent will fetch the official data and validate the company's active status.
