# Client Document Intake Skill

This skill handles the intake of legal documentation (Proof of ID, Proof of Address, Certificates of Incorporation) required from new clients to satisfy standard KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) requirements for London law firms.

## Usage
1. Guide the user through the document upload process.
2. Verify that uploaded documents are legible and check for expiry dates.
3. Store the intake state securely for the onboarding review process.
